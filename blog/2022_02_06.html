Recipe:
California Sazerac

Add 3 shakes of of Peychaud's into a shot glass and equal amounts of Herbsaint and Sazerac Rye until half full. Swirl to mix and pour down the inside of a chilled Sodastream liter bottle of fizzy-water. Cap and slowly invert to mix. Uncap. Enjoy. Taste of Mardi Gras, California spritzer aesthetic, and not so much sugar.
