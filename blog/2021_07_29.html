Ghostbusters v Exorcist

don’t pretend you won’t watch it

Ghostbusters v The Exorcist is coming to Disney’s new DarkHorse+ streaming service this fall. It features real-life exorcist Jason Momoa, who will be doing all his own stunts. Egon, Spengler, Venkman, et al will be played by the Little Rascals of the “Our Gang” classics, who are in fact ghosts themselves, brought back to life through cartoon magic, CGI deepfakery, and over the objections of all their living family members (who get bupkis). The soundtrack is composed by … wait for it … Bear McCreary, and there just might be an Easter egg after the closing credits. Act surprised, and don't forget to sign up. It's only $50/month.

It’s tough in Hollywood these days, having to compete with YouTube cat videos.
